Privacy policy

ChessiL.com is a chess website.

ChessiL.com will collect and use only the needed data to provide its services.

You may create an account to play rated games and keep a game history.

Accounts and all related data will be deleted after 1 year of inactivity.

Your data will not be shared with third parties.

IP address

Mandatory for establishing an internet connection.

Used to prevent cybernetic attacks and frauds.

Deleted after 1 year.

Session cookie

Given to you by ChessiL.com, keep it secret and safe.

Used to authenticate you and keep you logged in.

Used to play chess games and make moves as yourself.

Used to identify unregistered users.

Deleted after 1 week of inactivity or 1 year.

HTTP requests

Given to ChessiL.com by you.

Used to navigate the website.

Used to prevent cybernetic attacks and frauds.

Deleted after 1 year.

Username

Willingly given to ChessiL.com by you to create an account.

Used to identify yourself with ChessiL.com and other users.

Deleted along with account deletion.

Password

Willingly given to ChessiL.com by you to create an account.

Hashed with argon2id and never stored in plain text.

Used to log into your account.

Deleted along with account deletion.

Chess games and moves

Willingly given to ChessiL.com by you when playing games.

Used to display your previous games and to prevent cheating.

Anonymized along with account deletion.

Deleted when both players are anonymous.
